## About Me

Hi! I'm a master's student at AI4GC Lab, Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Shengyu Zhang. My work centers on **GUI agents**. I co-led **DAC-Bench**, a benchmark for long-horizon mobile GUI tasks with branching, decision-aware structures, which probes how agents plan over many steps and make the right choices at conditional branches.

I'm currently working on **self-generated trajectory data** for GUI agents — building a pipeline that automatically produces and annotates interaction trajectories to scale up training and evaluation data.

## Research Interests

- **GUI Agents** — long-horizon, branching task execution on real Android/iOS apps; benchmarking and decision-aware evaluation (DAC-Bench).
